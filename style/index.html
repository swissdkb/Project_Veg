About The Project

VEG DAPP was founded in Silicon Valley, the United States. Two laboratories in Silicon Valley and Israel gathered the top technology team of the block chain, and applied scenario concept on the block chain of global vertical ecological technology.

VEG (full name VERTICAL ECOLOGY GLOBAL) is positioned as a global public chain operation compatible system + decentralized super application DAPP + global community vertical integration of the co-construction ecosystem, in the formation of a common mechanism to promote global consensus.

Opening the VEG Block Chain Community Global Node Program, VEG makes full use of the BIG bandwidth, LOW delay and SUP connection capabilities of the 5G era to promote industry digitization and enable VEG vertical ecology.
